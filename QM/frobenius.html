Frobenius Method

The Frobenius method is used to find solutions to linear second-order differential equations near a regular singular point. We assume a solution of the form:

\( y(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} a_n x^{n + r} \)

The goal is to determine the indicial equation for \( r \), and then compute the recurrence relation for the coefficients \( a_n \).
